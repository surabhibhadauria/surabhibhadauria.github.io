Principal Investigator

Dr. Surabhi Bhadauria

Assistant Professor (Tenure-Track) · Aerospace Engineering · ERAU

Dr. Surabhi Bhadauria is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Aerospace Engineering at Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University, where she leads the Space Observation and Astrodynamics Research (SOAR) Laboratory. Her research focuses on Space Domain Awareness, cislunar surveillance, orbital mechanics and astrodynamics, sensor network design, and autonomous space systems for safe and sustainable space operations.

Education

  • Ph.D. in Aeronautical and Astronautical Engineering, Purdue University (2025)
  • M.S. in Aeronautical and Astronautical Engineering, Purdue University (2020)
  • B.E. in Materials and Metallurgical Engineering, Punjab Engineering College, India (2016)
