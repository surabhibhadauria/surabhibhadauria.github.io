FIRST Grant Awarded — Optimized Cislunar Observer Architectures

Dr. Bhadauria is awarded an ERAU Faculty Innovative Research in Science and Technology (FIRST) grant as sole PI. The project (July 2026 – June 2027) will develop optimized space-based observer architectures for sustained cislunar monitoring.

Three papers accepted at AAS/AIAA Astrodynamics Specialist Conference, Whistler

SOAR Lab members will present at the AAS/AIAA Astrodynamics Specialist Conference in Whistler, BC: constellation design for cislunar surveillance (Quan et al.), multi-sensor data fusion (Bottero, Lovell & Bhadauria), and attitude-orbital dynamics (Abdrabou, Crespo & Bhadauria).
