Join the Lab

We are building a team of curious, driven researchers excited about space safety and the future of orbital operations.

Why Join SDA Lab?

Our lab sits at the intersection of astrodynamics, estimation theory, and machine learning — a technically rich environment that prepares you for careers in government (NASA, DoD, Space Force), industry (SpaceX, Maxar, AGI/Ansys), or academia.

ERAU's Daytona Beach campus is one of the world's leading aviation and aerospace universities, with strong connections to government and commercial space. You'll have access to space-related coursework, seminars, and a community of aerospace researchers.

We value intellectual curiosity, rigor, and collaboration. Students in our lab publish their work, present at top conferences (AAS, AIAA), and build skills that are directly relevant to real-world space operations.

// How to Apply

Send an email to youremail@erau.edu with the subject line "SDA Lab — [Position Type] Application". Please include your CV, a brief statement of research interests (1 page max), and unofficial transcripts. PhD applicants should also apply through the ERAU graduate admissions portal.

// What We Look For
🧠

Technical Foundation

Strong math background (linear algebra, probability, differential equations). Experience in Python, MATLAB, or C++. Prior coursework in orbital mechanics or estimation theory is a plus but not required.

🔬

Research Drive

Genuine curiosity and motivation to push knowledge forward. Comfort with ambiguity and willingness to try things that might not work. Reading papers, asking questions, and thinking independently.

🤝

Collaboration

We are a team. Clear communication, willingness to help others, and openness to feedback are as important to us as technical skill. The lab environment is supportive and inclusive.
