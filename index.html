// What We Do

Research Thrusts

Our work spans sensor fusion, orbital mechanics, and machine learning — all in service of a safer space environment.

🛰️

Resident Space Object Tracking

Developing novel algorithms for initial orbit determination, state estimation, and data association for catalogued and uncatalogued RSOs in LEO, MEO, and GEO regimes.

📡

Sensor Fusion & Tasking

Fusing heterogeneous sensor data — radar, optical, laser ranging — to maximize surveillance coverage and orbit accuracy under realistic sensor constraints.

🤖

ML for Space Situational Awareness

Applying deep learning and Bayesian inference to object characterization, maneuver detection, and conjunction risk assessment at scale.
