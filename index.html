// What We Do

Research Thrusts

Our work spans cislunar astrodynamics, observer optimization, and multi-sensor fusion — in service of sustained awareness beyond Earth orbit.

🌙

Cislunar Space Domain Awareness

Developing surveillance frameworks for the Earth-Moon system using the Bicircular Restricted Four-Body Problem (BCR4BP) geometry to identify and exploit optimal observation regions.

🔭

Observer Architecture & Trajectory Design

Optimizing placement and orbits of ground- and space-based observers for sustained cislunar monitoring, including constellation design and halo/resonance orbit utilization.

📡

Multi-Sensor Fusion for SSA

Fusing radar and optical observations to enhance detection and tracking of resident space objects throughout the cislunar regime, including near-lunar and deep-space trajectories.
