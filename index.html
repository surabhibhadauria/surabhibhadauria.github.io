Research Thrusts

Our work spans cislunar astrodynamics, observer optimization, sensor fusion, and space debris mitigation — in service of sustained awareness beyond Earth orbit.

🔭

Observer Architecture & Trajectory Design

Designing next-generation sensor networks and surveillance satellite constellations for persistent Space Domain Awareness in the Earth-Moon system.

📡

Multi-Sensor Data Fusion for SSA

Combining optical, radar, and laser-ranging observations into a unified operational picture for improved orbit determination and space object tracking.

🛸

Space Debris Mitigation & Flight Experiments

Investigating plasma-based debris removal techniques and developing optical payloads for space-based SDA through international student flight missions.
